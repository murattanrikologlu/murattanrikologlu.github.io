Image & AI Processing

When using Vidox's AI product photography and video generation features, the photos you upload are temporarily processed by our AI systems to transform your product images into professional studio shots, scenic compositions, or animated product videos. This processing applies the selected AI scene, style, or animation effect to your input image.

The Application does not collect, store, or retain any biometric identifiers, facial recognition data, or any data that could be used to identify a person. On-device image analysis (such as automatic background detection for product cutout) is performed locally on your device using Apple's Vision framework and is not transmitted to our servers.

All uploaded source photos are stored securely on our servers only for processing purposes and are automatically deleted within 24 hours. AI-generated outputs (your final product photos and videos) are stored as part of your generation history and remain available for you to access, share, or delete at any time. No source images are permanently stored, shared with third parties for purposes unrelated to generating your requested content, sold, or used to train AI models without your explicit consent.
