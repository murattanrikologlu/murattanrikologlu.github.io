Face Data & AI Processing

When using AI Photo Editor’s AI filters, AI templates, or AI-generated portrait/headshot features, the photos you upload are temporarily processed only to detect basic facial key points (such as the approximate positions of the eyes, nose, and mouth). This detection is used solely to correctly apply the selected AI effect or style to your image.

The Application does not collect, store, or retain any biometric identifiers, facial geometry, or any face data that could be used to identify a person. Facial key point detection is performed automatically for visual processing only and is not linked to your identity, device information, or account.

All uploaded photos and their associated temporary facial key point data are stored securely on our servers only for processing purposes and are automatically deleted within 7 days. No images or facial data are permanently stored, shared with third parties, sold, or used for any purpose other than generating the requested AI effect.
